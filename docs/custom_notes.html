Move Note

Note pages created by YourNotes can be pinned on everywhere on the screen. Simply drag the handle at the top of pages and move your mouse to change the position of your notes displayed on the screen.



Resize Notes

Also, you can change the size of note pages. Simply drag the handle at the bottom-left corner of pages and move your mouse to change the size of your notes.



Change Background Color

YourNotes provides colorful note pages. Here are two ways for you to change the background of your notes.


1.Click the button in shape of triangle on the bottom-left corner of pages to switch background among provided colors.

2.Right-click at the note page, choose [Background...] menu item, and select the background color in the popup menu.


Please note that note pages without focus is displayed translucently.
